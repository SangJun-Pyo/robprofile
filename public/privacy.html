Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Roblox Archetype ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Roblox Username: When you use our analysis tool, you enter a Roblox username to analyze.
  • Contact Information: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and message content.

2.2 Information from Roblox (Public Data)

For quick analysis, we access only publicly available information from Roblox profiles, including:

  • Public profile information (username, display name, account age)
  • Public badges earned
  • Public group memberships
  • Avatar thumbnail (public image)

2.3 Information from Roblox OAuth (With Your Consent)

When you choose to use the full analysis feature and authorize our application through Roblox OAuth, we request access to the following scopes with your explicit consent:

  • openid: Basic authentication to verify your identity
  • profile: Your Roblox user ID, username, display name, and profile URL

How we use OAuth data:

  • To provide personalized playstyle analysis
  • To verify your account ownership for accurate results
  • To display your profile information in your analysis results

Data retention: OAuth tokens are stored only for the duration of your session. We do not store your OAuth access tokens or refresh tokens permanently. Your analysis results are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

2.4 What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect or request access to:

  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Private messages or friend lists
  • Transaction history or Robux balance
  • Email addresses or personal identification from Roblox
  • Any private profile information beyond the scopes listed above
  • Ability to take actions on your behalf

2.5 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect standard log information such as IP addresses, browser type, and access times for security and analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide our playstyle analysis service
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Ensure the security of our platform

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. Analysis results are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. Contact form submissions are handled through Formspree, a third-party service with its own privacy practices.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service uses the following third-party services:

  • Roblox API & OAuth 2.0: To fetch profile data for analysis and authenticate users who opt for full analysis
  • Formspree: To process contact form submissions
  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements
  • Cloudflare: For hosting and security

Each of these services has their own privacy policies that govern their data practices.

6. Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies for essential functionality such as saving your language preference and theme choice. Google AdSense may use cookies to display personalized advertisements based on your browsing history. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
  • Revoke OAuth access: You can revoke our application's access to your Roblox account at any time through your Roblox account settings under "Connected Apps" or "Third-Party Apps"

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
